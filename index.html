Welcome to the WUSTL CIG's DL-PTED Project!

Deep learning may revolutionize the study of musculoskeletal conditions by automating the evaluation pipeline. This project seeks to develop tissue and modality-specific DL algorithms for automatic evaluation of disease stage in post-traumatic elbow conditions using histology, computed tomography, and magnetic resonance imaging. This project is in collaboration with WashU's Musculoskeletal Soft Tissue Laboratory.
